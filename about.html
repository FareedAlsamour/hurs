About Aliens


Aliens now a valuable resource for people who want to make the most of their devices. Our Portal was started in 2010 as a way to showcase the groundbreaking development and discussions going on about Android, Windows Mobile, and more. Now we have over 10,000 articles dealing with Phones, Tablets, Wearables, Reviews, and mobile industry news. Our writers hail from all over the spectrum of mobile technology, from geeks and professionals (some even interchangeably) to hobbyists, and are devoted to providing you with the best possible coverage of Mobile news and community discussion.
